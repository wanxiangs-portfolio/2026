I am a Senior Content and Data Specialist at SAYS.com under REV Media / Media Prima, working across editorial, analytics, video, social media, and AI automation. My editorial expertise spans social news, politics, personal finance, global events, explainers, and human interest stories. Beyond writing, I identify and develop stories with strong cross-medium potential, including the "Malaysian Stories" video series that generated more than 20 million views within a year.

I lead SAYS' editorial analytics initiatives, producing 30+ in-depth reports over the past 5 years and developing a Gemini API-powered master sheet that provides content recommendations, automates reporting, surfaces performance insights, generates weekly summaries, and identifies reposting opportunities. These data-driven improvements contributed to up to 20% growth in page views for individual writers.

My role combines creative storytelling with data-driven decision-making. Despite managing dual responsibilities across content production and analytics, I have consistently achieved page view targets while driving data-driven initiatives that improve editorial efficiency and audience growth. I have also taken on leadership responsibilities, including managing and mentoring an executive within the Growth team.

Throughout my career, I have focused on working smarter by continuously learning and adapting to new tools and technologies. I independently developed my data analytics capabilities, combining editorial instincts with analytics, automation, and AI to improve workflows, uncover insights, and make better content decisions. My efforts in this area, including my contributions to GEO products, have also earned recognition from the company's CEO, COO, and CTO.

Outside SAYS, I bring over 10 years of freelance marketing experience across META Ads, Google Search Ads, TikTok Ads, YouTube Ads, AI-generated retargeting videos, content strategy, social media planning, reporting, and client creative direction - skills that continue to strengthen my approach to audience growth and content performance in my full-time role.
